About Me

My name is Joe. I love good food and a close game. I'm from a town called Syracuse, Utah. I've lived in Ohio for a couple years and it made me love Utah even more. The mountains are my fave. I love building and fixing things. It's such a good feeling to see when something comes together.

I love to learn the new technology! If it's new, I wanna learn it and I wanna see it in action. I'm all about pushing the envelope and experiencing with new ideas. To be honest, most every lesson I have learned has come from some kind of failure. When things break, you fix them. When you fix them, you learn.
